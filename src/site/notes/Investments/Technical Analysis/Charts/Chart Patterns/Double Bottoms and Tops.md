**Double Bottom** indicates a strong support level where price rebounds twice before moving up.  
**Double Top** indicates strong resistance where price peaks twice before moving down.

- **Mechanics**: Pattern confirmed when price breaks above the resistance (bottom) or below the support (top).
    
- **Usage**: Signals potential trend reversal or entry/exit points.
